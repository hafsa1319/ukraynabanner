Free German courses and language cafe for refugees from Ukraine
One of the first steps to life in Germany is learning the language. That is why we offer free introductory German courses and Sprachcafe to refugees from Ukraine. 
What you'll learn: 

Communicating in everyday situations, e.g. buying a train ticket.
Writing down contact information and filling out simple forms.
Having simple conversations in German. 

What is a language cafe?

It is therefore a place where people come together to exchange ideas and thereby refresh or acquire their language skills. Above all, the Sprachcafe serves to expand knowledge in addition to the actual lessons.
